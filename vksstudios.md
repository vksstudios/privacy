# Privacy Policy for VKS Studios

**Last Updated:** March 27, 2026

VKS Studios respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information across our apps, websites, and digital products.

## 1. Information Collection

We only collect information that is necessary for our services:
*   **User Identity:** Your name, email, and social login profile (e.g., Google OAuth).
*   **Direct Interaction:** Information you provide when contacting our support.
*   **Service Analytics:** Anonymous usage statistics to improve our app performance.

## 2. Information Use

We use your data for:
*   Providing and maintaining our digital services.
*   Communicating with you about account status or updates.
*   Improving our UI/UX across all VKS Studios products.

## 3. Data Protection

*   We use industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit.
*   We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party tools like Google Firebase, Google Identity, or standard analytics providers. We recommend checking their respective privacy policies as well.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information stored with VKS Studios. For most products, this can be done directly through the app settings or by contacting us.
